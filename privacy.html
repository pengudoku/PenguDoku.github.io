Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2, 2026

PenguDoku is operated by IdleHands Studio ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use PenguDoku.

PenguDoku is a single-player puzzle game for iOS. The game does not require an account, login, username, profile, online multiplayer, chat, or leaderboard.

1. Information We Collect

PenguDoku does not collect personal information through the app.

We do not require you to provide your name, email address, phone number, account information, location, photos, contacts, or payment information to play the game.

2. Local Game Data

PenguDoku may save limited game data locally on your device so the game can function properly.

This may include things like your game progress, level progress, puzzle state, lives or similar gameplay resources, and settings.

This data is stored on your device. It is not sent to IdleHands Studio, stored on our servers, sold, rented, or shared with third parties.

If you delete the app, your local game data may also be deleted.

3. No Ads, Analytics, or Online Tracking

PenguDoku does not use advertising networks, third-party analytics, crash reporting tools, Firebase, Apple Game Center, iCloud syncing, subscriptions, or in-app purchases.

We do not use PenguDoku to track you across other apps or websites.

4. Children's Privacy

PenguDoku is intended for a general audience and is not specifically directed to children under 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since PenguDoku does not collect personal information through the app, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

5. Apple App Store

PenguDoku is distributed through Apple's App Store. Apple may collect or process information related to app downloads, device settings, App Store usage, or other Apple services according to Apple's own privacy practices.

IdleHands Studio does not control Apple's data practices.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if PenguDoku changes or if legal requirements change.

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will change the Effective Date above. Your continued use of PenguDoku after an updated Privacy Policy becomes available means you accept the updated policy.
